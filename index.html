How to make business trips more sustainamble

Minimize air travel

Look for flight alternatives

Take the train or opt for virtual meetings.

Book direct flights

Reduce time in the air and fuel consumption

Book economy fares

They produce less carbon emissions than premium fares

Leave your `just in caese`outfit at home

Lighter luggage equals a lighter plane, which saves on emissions
Plan effectively

Combine meetings

Group several appointments together on one business trip.

Public transport

Where allowed and feasible, use safe public transportation or carpool with your colleagues.

If your rent a car, choose an electric vehicle.
